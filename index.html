About Me

Hello! My name is Jessica. I am a motivated individual passionate about continuous learning and growth. I enjoy connecting with others and making a positive impact in everything I do. As a graduate student, I have learned that there is an art to helping others while growing personaly and professionally. Work-life balance is essential for me to live a life that seems rewarding and enjoyable. Whether I am learning a new skill or relaxing with friends, I am intentional about keeping a positive attitude. While this is sometimes a challenge, it is not only rewarding but also brings me joy to encourage others around me to maintain or develop a positive attitude. I feel that just as any other human being I bring something unique to the table. That's why I focus on growing as an individual and developing a personal brand. It is no secret that a lot of people can either learn a skill or how to perform a job. However, not everyone can bring the combination of skills, life experience, and unique perspective that I have to offer. Think about it this way. Why do people love scrolling through social media regularly? The real reason is because everyone has something to offer that we have never encountered before in life. Understanding this way of thinking is just the start of understanding more about who I am. Explore more exciting things about me across this site!
